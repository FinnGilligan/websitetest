Outreach

Dawgma: FRC Team 1712 Outreach

Our robotics team focuses on making STEM accessible and exciting through various initiatives, including:

  • Collaborating each fall with JCHAI to teach robotics and engineering principles.
  • Supporting local FIRST LEGO League (FLL) teams through mentorship and coaching.
  • Hosting booths at the school district's STEM night to engage the community.
  • Assisting other FRC teams such as Team 9094 with technical support and guidance.

Technology Student Association (TSA)

As a member of TSA, I participated in volunteer efforts mainly related to FLL by helping judge and organize local competitions alongside other members of TSA and Dawgma.

Computer Science Club

  • Host CodeLM, a coding competition for high school students where we write problems and volunteer at the event.
  • Run program.it, exposing underserved students to computer science through educational outreach.
  • Lead CS1 Day, showcasing real-world applications of computer science to beginner programmers at our school.

Math Club

  • Coordinated a math enrichment program for middle school students in partnership with Villanova University.
  • Help organize and run LIMT, an annual math competition for middle schoolers where I have written problems, managed registrations, and graded papers.
